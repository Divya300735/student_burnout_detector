Statistical Insights

🔍 Key Finding 1: Sleep Duration

Students sleeping less than 5 hours show significantly higher burnout risk compared to those sleeping 7+ hours.

🔍 Key Finding 2: Stress Level

Stress level is the strongest predictor of burnout, with high correlation across all burnout indicators.

🔍 Key Finding 3: Screen Time

Excessive screen time (>8 hours) significantly increases burnout risk and sleep disruption.

🔍 Key Finding 4: Physical Activity

Regular physical activity is protective against burnout, showing inverse correlation with risk scores.
