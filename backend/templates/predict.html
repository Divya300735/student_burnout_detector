How the Calculator Works

Burnout Score Formula

BurnoutScore = 0.35 × stress_level + 0.25 × screen_time + 0.20 × study_hours - 0.30 × sleep_hours - 0.05 × physical_activity + 0.05 × assignment_load

The score is normalized to a 0-10 scale using min-max normalization based on dataset statistics.

Component Weights

  • Stress Level: 35% - Strongest burnout factor
  • Screen Time: 25% - Digital fatigue contributor
  • Study Hours: 20% - Academic workload indicator
  • Sleep Hours: -30% - Protective factor (negative)
  • Physical Activity: -5% - Protective factor (negative)
  • Assignment Load: +5% - Workload pressure
