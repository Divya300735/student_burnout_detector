How It Works

📊 Data Collection

Students provide lifestyle data: sleep hours, study hours, screen time, stress level, physical activity, and assignment load.

📈 Statistical Analysis

Our system uses correlation analysis, descriptive statistics, and regression models to identify burnout patterns.

⚠️ Risk Assessment

Calculate burnout score and categorize risk level (Low, Moderate, High) with personalized recommendations.

Burnout Score Formula

BurnoutScore =

0.35 × stress_level + 0.25 × screen_time + 0.20 × study_hours - 0.30 × sleep_hours - 0.05 × physical_activity + 0.05 × assignment_load

Normalized to 0-10 scale where 0-4 = Low Risk, 4-7 = Moderate Risk, 7-10 = High Risk
