📋 Risk Categories Explained

🟢 Low Risk (0–4)

Healthy lifestyle balance maintained. Students in this category show good stress management, adequate sleep, and balanced study habits.

🟡 Moderate Risk (4–7)

Some burnout indicators present. Students may experience moderate stress levels, irregular sleep patterns, or excessive screen time.

🔴 High Risk (7–10)

Significant burnout risk detected. Students show high stress levels, poor sleep quality, and unhealthy study-life balance.
